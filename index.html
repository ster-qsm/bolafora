About Football

Football, also known as soccer, is a sport played between two teams of eleven players. It is played in almost every country and is loved by millions of fans around the world.

History of Football

Modern football started in England in the 19th century. Over time, it became an international sport, with competitions like the FIFA World Cup bringing nations together.
